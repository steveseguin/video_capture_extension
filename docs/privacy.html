Our Commitment to Privacy

The VDO.Ninja Video Capture Extension is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Information Collection

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history or habits
  • Video content or metadata
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Any form of tracking cookies

What the Extension Stores Locally

The extension stores minimal data locally in your browser only:

  • Active stream sessions: Temporary data about your current streaming sessions (deleted when streams end)
  • User preferences: Your quality settings (bitrate, codec, etc.)
  • Stream metadata: Basic information like stream IDs and timestamps

All this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

How Video Streaming Works

Peer-to-Peer Technology

The extension uses WebRTC technology to establish direct peer-to-peer connections between you and your viewers. This means:

  • Video streams go directly from your browser to the viewer's browser
  • No video content passes through VDO.Ninja servers
  • No recording or storage of your streams occurs
  • Connections are encrypted end-to-end

TURN/STUN Servers

In some network configurations, TURN or STUN servers may be used to help establish connections:

  • These servers only relay encrypted traffic when direct P2P connection isn't possible
  • They cannot see or store the content of your streams
  • No logs are kept of relay usage

Permissions Explained

Why We Need Certain Permissions

  • Access to all websites: To detect and capture videos from any webpage you visit
  • Tab access: To capture your entire browser tab when you choose that option
  • Storage: To save your preferences and active streams locally

These permissions are used only for the stated functionality and only when you explicitly interact with the extension.

Third-Party Services

VDO.Ninja

The extension uses the open-source VDO.Ninja SDK for WebRTC functionality. VDO.Ninja:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not require account creation
  • Is open-source and privacy-focused
  • Only facilitates peer-to-peer connections

Data Security

Your privacy and security are protected through:

  • End-to-end encryption of all WebRTC streams
  • No external data transmission except P2P video streams
  • Local-only storage of preferences
  • No user accounts or authentication required
  • Open-source code available for review on GitHub

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • All local data can be cleared by removing the extension
  • You can stop any stream at any time
  • No data persistence after uninstallation
  • Full transparency through open-source code

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The extension does not collect any personal information from any users.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page and reflected in the extension's version history on GitHub.

Contact Information

For privacy concerns or questions about this policy:

  • Open an issue on GitHub
  • Review the source code at our repository

Open Source Commitment

This extension is fully open source. You can review every line of code to verify our privacy practices. We believe transparency is the best privacy policy.
