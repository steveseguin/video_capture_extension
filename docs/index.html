⚠️ DRM Limitations

This extension cannot capture DRM-protected content. Videos from Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu and similar services will appear as black screens due to browser security restrictions.

✅ Works with: YouTube, Discord, Twitch, Vimeo, and most non-DRM video content
❌ Does NOT work with: Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu, and other DRM-protected services

🎬 Video Detection

Automatically detects video elements on non-DRM webpages including YouTube, Discord, Twitch, and more.

📡 P2P Streaming

Stream directly to viewers using WebRTC peer-to-peer connections. No server processing required.

🖥️ Tab Capture

Capture and stream your entire browser tab with audio, perfect for presentations and tutorials.

⚙️ Quality Settings

Control bitrate, codec, resolution, and other streaming parameters for optimal performance.

🔒 Privacy Focused

All streaming is peer-to-peer. No data collection, no analytics, no server-side video processing.

🎨 Clean Interface

Intuitive dark theme interface that integrates seamlessly with your browser.
